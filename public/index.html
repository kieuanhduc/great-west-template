Your Premium Produce Partner

Sixty years ago our founder, Art Villa, set out to source the freshest fruits and vegetables, believing that our customers deserved premium produce at a fair price. Three generations later, the team at Great West Produce is still serving up the best in the industry and sharing our family’s love of quality products with a commitment to safety and sustainable environmental practices. From tangerines to leafy greens and everything in between, Great West is your produce partner.

A Committed Partnership

Being your partner in produce isn’t just something we say, it’s what we embody through the actions we take. As a family-run operation, we care about the communities we serve and it is our commitment to ensure that the produce we deliver is handled responsibly for the consumer and the environment. We make sure to uphold the highest industry standards in safe food practices and environmental sustainability so that our communities can enjoy the freshest fruits and vegetables.
